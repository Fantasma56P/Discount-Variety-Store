# Discount-Variety-Store
Here, we see the sales of a fictional store over the years and months, along with insights such as growth, average sales, the lowest sales month, and the highest sales month.

Tools used: Google Sheets.
Project Overview : In this project, we aimed to analyze our fictional store's monthly and yearly sales, average sales, and annual growth rates.

Steps Followed:
1 - Calculate the total annual sales revenue
2 - Identify the difference between years
3 - Calculate the growth rate
4 - Find the most profitable months

Key Findings:
The periods with the highest annual sales are the summer months and December, while the lowest sales have been recorded in January.
